Story behind this platform

Growing up in Nepal, I only had one library in my town: it housed a collection of dusty, ancient books, none of which I was interested in reading. I always dreamed of building the largest library with the largest collection of books in my village in Nepal, and during this hackathon, Anthony and I had the idea of using QR codes as a way of building this library on a digital scale. Through this platform, users can print QR codes and create a library anywhere. This allows them to conveniently access books that are hosted in the cloud.
